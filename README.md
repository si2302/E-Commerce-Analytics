# E-Commerce-Analytics
📌 Project Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of e-commerce sales data to help stakeholders monitor performance and make data-driven decisions.

It focuses on:

📈 Sales Growth by Month

💰 Profit Growth by Month

🚚 Shipping Mode Analysis

📦 Sub-Category Wise Sales & Profit

🌍 Country-wise Growth

📊 Shipping Cost Distribution by Segment

🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to:

Monitor monthly sales and profit performance

Identify high-performing product categories

Analyze customer segments and shipping preferences

Track regional and country-wise growth

Understand cost distribution across segments

📈 Key Insights

Sales show consistent growth, with peak performance in the final quarter.

Profit trends closely follow sales patterns, indicating stable margins.

Standard Class shipping is the most frequently used shipping mode.

The Consumer segment contributes the largest share of shipping costs.

Some sub-categories generate high sales but comparatively lower profit, indicating margin improvement opportunities.

A few countries contribute significantly higher revenue than others.
